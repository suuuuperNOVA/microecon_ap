Recap

Factors of Production

  • Land (paid by Rent)

  • Labor (paid by Wage)

  • Capital (paid by Interest)

    • Physical capital
      Any human-made resource that is used to create other goods and services.

    • Human capital
      Any skills or knowledge gained by a worker through education and experience.

  • Entrepreneurship (paid by Profit)

    In the product market, individuals (consumers) pay businesses for goods and services.
    In the factor market, businesses (consumers) pay individuals for the use of resources.

Definitions

Derived demand

Demand for the factors of production because this demand is derived from goods that are produced by these inputs.

Monopsony

Occurs when there is a single buyer of labor.

Monopoly of the factor market.

Similar to monopolies, monopsonies underhires and pays workers less than in a competitive market.
